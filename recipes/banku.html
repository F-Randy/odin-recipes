Banku

Description

Banku is a traditional Ghanaian dish made from fermented corn and cassava dough

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of cassava dough
  • 1 cup of corn dough
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Fetch equal portions of cassva and corn dough
  2. Add enough water and mix well until smooth concistency is achieved
  3. Add Salt and steam the mixture for about 30 minutes or until cooked through
  4. Stir the mixture while on fire to avoid lumps and ensure even cooking
  5. Dish the cooked banku onto a clean surface
  6. Serve with soup or stew of your choice
